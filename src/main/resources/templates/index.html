Made for job seekers, by job seekers

Job hunting sucks. We get it.

We've all been there before. You need a job, so you start sending out your resume far and wide to hopefully land a job. Days turn to weeks and you begin going on a application spree again because now you're getting worried. Before you know it you've sent 100 applications! You need help to keep track of all of them and to set yourself apart from everyone else

How we can help you

AppliTraq gives you the resources to keep track of your job applications and more! We provide

  • A Kanban board to keep track of all job applications and what stage they are in
  • Ability to create notes such as how the interview went or things to work on for this position
  • Send yourself an email with our reminder feature! Keep track of it in your emails to remind yourself of something
  • See a timeline showing when you created the job application. This allows you to see if an application has gone too long without an update so you can send a follow up email
